Our Training Programs

We offer specialized classes to build complete MMA fighters through focused, systematic training.

Striking Classes

Master the art of long-range combat through disciplined, progressive training.

Beginner Striking | Earn fundamental techniques including basic punches, kicks, knees, and elbows. Build proper form, footwork, and defensive fundamentals in a supportive environment.

Advanced Striking | Develop complex combinations, advanced defensive techniques, counter-attacking strategies, and fight IQ for competitive situations.

Grappling Classes

Excel in close-range combat and ground fighting through technical mastery.

Beginner Grappling | Learn essential takedowns, basic submissions, positional control, and escape fundamentals. Perfect for building a strong grappling foundation.

Advanced Grappling | Master complex submission chains, advanced transitions, wrestling combinations, and competition-level grappling strategies.

MMA Integration Sessions

Beginner MMA | Learn to connect striking and grappling through structured drills and situational sparring. Develop confidence with transitions, takedown entries, and controlled ground striking in a safe environment.

Advanced MMA | Push your skills further with complex drills, situational work, and full sparring. Focus on fluid combinations between striking and grappling, cage control, and competition-level strategies.

Our Training Philosophy

We believe in teaching striking and grappling as separate disciplines initially to prevent cognitive overload and ensure technical mastery. This focused approach allows students to develop proper muscle memory and deep understanding of each art before integrating them into complete MMA proficiency.

By mastering individual components first, fighters build stronger foundations and develop more effective integrated skills when they begin combining techniques in our MMA sessions.
