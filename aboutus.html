About Coach Michael

With over 15 years of professional fighting and coaching experience, Coach Michael has dedicated his life to mastering and teaching mixed martial arts.

Professional Background

Former professional MMA fighter with a record of 12-3-0. Competed in regional promotions across the country before transitioning to full-time coaching.

Credentials & Achievements

  • Black Belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu under Professor John Danaher
  • Level 3 Muay Thai Certification from International Federation
  • USA Wrestling Copper Certified Coach
  • Trained 5 regional champions and 2 national competitors

Coaching Philosophy

"I believe every fighter has unique potential. My job is to unlock that potential through technical precision, strategic thinking, and mental toughness. Whether you're looking to compete or just get in the best shape of your life, I'll help you reach your goals."
